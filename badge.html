How to Submit Your Research

Step 1: Select Your User Type

Choose whether you are an LPU student or a non-LPU student. This helps us categorize your research appropriately.

Step 2: Fill Personal Information

Enter your full name, student number (if applicable), campus, and program/department details accurately.

Step 3: Enter Research Details

Provide your thesis title and upload your research document in the supported format (PDF, DOC, or DOCX).

Step 4: Earn Achievements

Return regularly to the system to earn badges. Bronze at 3 visits, Silver at 6 visits, and Gold at 10 visits.
